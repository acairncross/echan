Basics

What is eikenchan?

eikenchan is a simple image-based bulletin board where anyone can post comments and share images. eikenchan's collaborative-community format is copied from one of the most popular forums in America, 4chan. A board is dedicated to different topics, from Japanese anime, manga and culture to videogames, musics, and photography. Users do not need to register a username before participating in the community.

What should I know before I post?

Users are encouraged to participate actively in our community, but only after they have familiarised themselves with the rules (there aren't any) and this FAQ. Breaking the rules will not result in post deletion, a temporary ban or permanent banishment because there aren't any rules.

How do I post anonymously?

To post as "Anonymous", simply do not fill in the [Name] field when submitting content. Information such as your personal IP address is not even viewable to the administrator because I don't know how.

Can I register a username?

No user registration process is available. If you're worried about somebody imporsonating you, consider using a tripcode to help validate your identity.

Must I post an image?

To start a new thread, you must attach an image. eikenchan is primarily an imageboard after all! When replying to an already existing thread, no attachment is required.

Can I reply with an image?

Yes. To reply to a thread with an image of your own, click the [Reply] link and fill in the post box as you normally would, making sure to specify an image for attachment.

How do I quote somebody?

To quote a portion of text, simple place a pointer (">") in front of the text you wish to highlight (ex. ">This is a quote"). To link to and highlight an entire post, place two pointers in front of its unique post number (ex. ">>210981").

How do I use a "tripcode"?

Tripcodes can help verify a user's identity to others, and are a type of pseudo-registration. To use a normal tripcode, place a hash mark ("#") followed by a word of short phrase after what you've entered into the [Name] field (ex. "User#password"). Upon submission, the server will generate the hash unique to that particular word or phrase. The previous example would display User !2TPP8kKr4G after being posted.


Important note: A tripcode is generated only using the text entered after the hash mark. Your entered name, IP address, cookie information, etc. do not affect the output. Tripcodes are not secure; they can be cracked with relative ease.

Culture

Who is "Eiken"?

Me.

What are "GETs"?

"GETs" happen on the approach to significant or unique post numbers on the board (ex. "2GET", "11GET", etc.). GETs are sought after by many, and normally multiple users will compete for one, often posting quite quickly in order to better their chances of achieving the desired GET number.

Technical

What software does eikenchan user?

eikenchan runs a Python web framework called webapp2. It is derived from webapp. Currently there are no plans to make our code available to the public (although I've got no problem doing so if someone asks for it).

What hardware does eikenchan run on?

eikenchan is probably powered by one server, and averages just over 0Gbps in bandwidth throughput. (To be precise, average throughput is about 0.1 gigabits per day.)

What personal information is collected?

eikenchan does not collect user information for postings. Once a post is pruned or deleted, it is removed from our web server. We value the privacy of our users, and will not make private information such as IP addresses available to others, except to comply with court orders or to cooperate with law enforcement agencies when appropriate.

About

When did eikenchan open?

eikenchan opened to the public on Christmas Day 2012. Initially, it only supported text-based posts, and lacked many other popular features such as threads and tripcodes.
