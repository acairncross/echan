What is eikenchan?

eikenchan is a simple image-based bulletin board where anyone can post comments and share images. There is a board dedicated to a variety of topics, from Japanese animation and culture to videogames, music, and photography. Users do not need to register an account before participating in the community. Feel free to click on the board that interest you and jump right in!


Be sure to read the FAQ if you wish to learn more.
